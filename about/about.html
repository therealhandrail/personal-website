About Me!

I was born right here in Lewsiton, Maine! I'm a huge music nerd
I play in two bands, one is an indie-punk band called Sorry Safari,
and I have a few other projects that include Disco covers, hardcore
punk, and country.

Aside from music, I am a full-time graphic designer, where I design
logos, branding, magazines, and display ads. I have been getting into
real gamer nerd stuff lately, playing the likes of Dota 2, Ghost of Tsushima,
and Baldur's Gate.

My favorite food is Ramen, when I travel, I made it my mission to try all the
ramen places, in hopes to find the Ramen that will change my life. For little
hobbies, I love to draw (even though I am quite bad), I play men's league hockey
as a goalie, and I ski every day that it's "too dangerous to drive to the office"

As for my coding experience, I taught myself HTML and CSS around covid,
as graphic design jobs were few and far between. I fell in love instatly
and knew that I would love to make a career out of it someday.

I feel this website is the limit of my coding knowledge. I tried to learn
Javascript on my own but it was very hard knowing where to start. I'm hoping
with this class I will have that 'guided learning' experience. I definitely
love to do research on my own, but I feel I need to be pointed in the right
direction at times. Also, I can copy Script and have it run, but I struggle
when it comes to writing script, because my brain just doesnt have that
programming mind yet.
